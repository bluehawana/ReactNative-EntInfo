Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to 2watchhub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, or email, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo as provided by your authentication provider. This information is used solely to identify your account and sync your watchlist.

Watchlist Data

If you are signed in, your watchlist is stored in Google Cloud Firestore to enable cross-device sync. If you are not signed in, your watchlist is stored locally on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous information about how you use the app to improve user experience
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes
  • Crash Data: Anonymous crash reports may be sent to help us fix bugs and improve stability

Third-Party Services

2watchhub uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google) — Used for authentication and cloud data storage. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

TMDB (The Movie Database) API — Used to fetch movie and TV show information. TMDB's privacy policy: themoviedb.org/privacy-policy

Streaming Provider Links — Links to third-party streaming services (Netflix, Disney+, etc.). Each service has its own privacy policy. 2watchhub does not track your activity on these external platforms.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Sync your watchlist across devices
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. We do not share data with other companies, marketing agencies, or data brokers.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect any data processed through our app, including encryption in transit and secure cloud storage via Google Firebase.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by signing out and contacting us. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

2watchhub is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Advertising

2watchhub does not display third-party advertisements.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

We commit to transparency about data usage, minimal data collection principles, and providing users control over their data.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

2watchhub does not sell personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
